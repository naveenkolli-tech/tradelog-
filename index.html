📐 How The Math Works

Dozen Bet Payout: 2:1 (bet $10, win → profit $20)
Break-even WR: 33.4% (our system gates at 90%+ confidence)
Base Bet: 3% of bankroll (conservative, survives 10+ losses)
Recovery: Modified ladder (NOT Martingale — gradual increase with cap)
Stop-Loss: -30% of bankroll (protects from ruin)
Take-Profit: +50% of bankroll (locks gains)

Why not Martingale? Martingale doubles after each loss (e.g. $3 → $6 → $12 → $24 → $48). Just 4 losses = $93 gone from a $100 bankroll. Our ladder increases gradually ($3 → $4 → $6 → $8 → $10 max) and caps at 10%, so 4 losses only costs $21, and one win at Level 3+ recovers the deficit.
